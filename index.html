Thanks for coming to our website. We'd love if you adopted one or more of our animals.

Here they are:

Jacob

Jacob is a lovely 3 year old pug from Scappoose, Oregon. He is loving, gets along well with parrots and cats. He is afraid of cars, but loves bicycles.

Anna

Anna hales from Guatemala. She enjoys talking up a storm and flying out of a warm latte every morning. Don't worry, she will return by lunch time for a nap.

Diesel

Diesel is a retired Iditarod race from Alaska. He is a 14 year old cat with thousands of miles under his paws. He is pretty specific when it comes to his diet, liking only wet food or freshly caught mice from southern France (don't ask us why). He will spend most of his days soaking up the sun via a south facing window.
